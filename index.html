Edit Trax NFTs are minted on the "clean" Tezos Blackchain. Find information about our custom smart contract here soon. To purchase Edit Trax players you'll need to have a crypto wallet with XTZ (easily accessible through Coinbase). XTZ is the native currency of the Tezos Blockchain. Follow the below steps to secure your crypto wallet with XTZ. *Recommendations only, do your own research

DOWNLOAD COINBASE APP

Link to Coinbase which allows you to deposit USD from a Bank and exchange it for crypto currency. You will need crypto with the ticker XTZ to purchase Edit Trax and other NFTs on the Tezos Blockchain.

TRANSFER USD TO COINBASE

Deposit USD into your account.

CONVERT USD TO XTZ IN COINBASE

Purchase XTZ at a price you choose to be good.

DOWNLOAD A TEZOS COMPATIBLE WALLET

Download a Temple OR Kukai wallet, required to make purchases on the Tezos Blockchain.

TRANSFER XTZ TO KUKAI OR TEMPLE WALLET

Send XTZ from Coinbase to your Temple or Kukai wallet. You can now make purchases when browsing art work and syncing your wallet at the time of purchase.
