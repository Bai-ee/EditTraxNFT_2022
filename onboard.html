GET TEZOS

To collect EditTrax players you'll need to have a crypto wallet with XTZ (easily accessible through Coinbase). XTZ is the native currency of the Tezos Blockchain, an the energy efficient Proof of Stake Protocol. Follow the below steps to secure your crypto wallet with XTZ. *Do your own research

DOWNLOAD COINBASE APP

Link to Coinbase which allows you to deposit USD from a Bank and exchange it for crypto currency. You will need crypto with the ticker XTZ to purchase EditTrax and other NFTs on the Tezos Blockchain.

TRANSFER USD TO COINBASE

Transfer from any major Bank directly into the Coinbase app.

CONVERT USD TO XTZ IN COINBASE

Purchase XTZ at a price you choose.

DOWNLOAD A TEZOS COMPATIBLE WALLET

Download a Temple OR Kukai wallet, required to make purchases on the Tezos Blockchain.

TRANSFER XTZ TO KUKAI OR TEMPLE WALLET

Send XTZ from Coinbase to your Temple or Kukai wallet. You can now make purchases when browsing art work and syncing your wallet at the time of purchase.
